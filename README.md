# 𓂀 KMT — Ancient Egyptian Tablet Reader

Upload a photo of hieroglyphs, a tablet, papyrus, or temple inscription and get an AI-powered translation with historical context.

## Features
- Upload any image of ancient Egyptian writing
- AI reads and translates the inscription
- Provides rich historical and cultural context
- Dark papyrus-themed UI

## How to Use
1. Open `hieroglyph-reader.html` in your browser
2. Upload a photo of hieroglyphs
3. Click **Invoke the Wisdom of Thoth**
4. Read your ancestors' words
